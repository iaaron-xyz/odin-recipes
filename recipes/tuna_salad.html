Tuna Salad

Description

This is a delicious tuna and lettuce salad with chopped eggs, and cucumber. Great with French dressing. Also, to vary this, you can try mayonnaise instead of French dressing and add garlic and onion powder.

ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) granbanzo beans, drained (optional)
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 cucumber peleed and diced
  • 1 head iceber lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • ½ cup French dressing

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine tuna, chickpeas (garbanzo beans), chopped hard-boiled eggs, diced cucumber, and lettuce pieces.
  2. Toss well and add French dressing to taste.
